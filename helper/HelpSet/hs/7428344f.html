"still possible - but really bad" Vectorlists

Many function in Vecci do not work with "still possible - but really bad" vectorlists. What makes a vectorlist only "still possible - but really bad"?

  • if there are many or all vectors "individual" vectors, that are not connected to any other vectors

  • if the vectorlist is perfect or good and contains very many vectors (e.g. >50)

  • all vectors have a length smaller than 127/128 (depending on the sign)

These vectors can only be exported to "synced" list (not if good or perfect - these lists just look bad on a real vectrex).

The sync list generated is a real performance "hog", since it will sync after each such vector.

Try to avoid these kind of vectorlists at all cost!
